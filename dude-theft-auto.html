About Dude Theft Auto

If you've ever thought of a game that is both an open-world sandbox and offers absolute freedom in gameplay, then Dude Theft Auto is the name that cannot be ignored. This is an action game with a fun style, where you can do almost anything: explore the city, drive cars, troll NPCs, participate in fierce gunfights or simply walk around and enjoy music in the middle of the street.

An open world full of freedom in Dude Theft Auto

Right at the start, you will appear in a small house and a peaceful neighborhood. But that's just the beginning, because out there is a large city waiting for you to explore. In Dude Theft Auto, you are not constrained by a linear plot. Instead, you are empowered to decide everything—from driving around the neighborhood to causing chaos throughout the city.

Rich and diverse gameplay

The game offers many game modes and large maps. Players can choose the experience that suits their style:

  • Free exploration: wander, chat or poke NPCs.
  • Causing chaos: use weapons, destroy the environment, and participate in dramatic gunfights.
  • Tasks: complete mini-quests or small tasks to earn money, then use the money to buy more weapons, vehicles or new equipment.

Chaos but full of humor

Dude Theft Auto's signature feature is the fun ragdoll physics mechanism. This makes every action, from jumps and collisions to being thrown away, funny. The game does not follow the tense and serious style of other action games but turns every moment into a humorous situation unexpectedly.

Game controls

  • WASD: move the character and control the car.
  • Mouse: aim and interact.

In addition, other function keys will support you in changing weapons or using items.

Reasons why Dude Theft Auto is worth playing

  • A large open world, full of freedom.
  • Diverse game modes and many different maps.
  • A rich vehicle system: bicycles, cars, tanks, helicopters...
  • Funny ragdoll physics mechanism, creating many funny situations.
  • A variety of weapons, from shotguns and RPGs to "weird" items to troll NPCs.
  • Small missions help the game always have more goals for players.

Play and relax right away with Dude Theft Auto.

Dude Theft Auto brings a unique open-world experience: no constraints, no specific rules, just fun and freedom. You can become a "street hero" or a troublemaker; it all depends on your choice. If you are looking for an entertaining action game that is both dramatic and humorous, then Dude Theft Auto will definitely satisfy you.
